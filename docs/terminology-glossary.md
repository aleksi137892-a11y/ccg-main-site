## Capitalization Rules

### Always Capitalize
- Instrument names: The Ledger, The List, Complicity Index
- Section names: Appeal, Record, Remedy
- Program names: Forum for Justice, Rustaveli Project
- Organization names: Civic Council of Georgia, IIM Georgia

### Lowercase in Prose
- Generic references: "the sanctions pathway," "a petition," "the dossier"
- Descriptive uses: "complicity tracking," "appeal process"

### Use "The" Article
- The Ledger (not just "Ledger")
- The List (not just "List")
- The Capture Engine (not just "Capture Engine")
- The Charter (not just "Charter")
